About Me

Hi, I’m Manish Sehgal, a passionate graphic designer with a sharp eye for detail and a love for visual storytelling. With 5 years of experience in branding, digital design, and print media, I help businesses and individuals bring their ideas to life through compelling, purpose-driven design. Whether I’m crafting a logo, building a visual identity, or designing marketing materials, my goal is always the same: to create thoughtful, visually striking work that communicates clearly and connects emotionally. When I’m not designing, you’ll usually find me sketching, exploring design trends, or getting inspired by art, photography, and everyday moments. Let’s create something beautiful together.
